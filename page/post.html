Trip to Napa

I spent a day trip to take my girl to the Napa Valley. It was a lot of fun with twisty roads up CA1

It was a sunny and warm day. Such a perfect condition for a cruise! When I passed the Golden Gate Bridge, I took a turn to go pass the Mount Tamalpais, heading toward CA 1. It is my most favorite road in this country
Then, after about 10 miles, I escaped from the shoreline and got back on CA 1, heading eastbound toward Napa Valley. This is an unsual route but it is much better than cruising on freeways all the time.
